AERO COMPOSITE CLUSTER

AIR FILTER

  • PART MODEL – HCS2021-01.
  • FUNCTION – The air filter in a composite repair hot bonder system serves to clean and purify the air that is supplied to the equipment, particularly when the system uses plant air for processes such as vacuum generation or hot air curing.
  • HOW IT WORKS – The air filter traps dust, particulates, oil, and moisture from the incoming air supply, ensuring that only clean, dry air enters the system. This is crucial for maintaining the quality of the composite repair and preventing contamination of the repair area or sensitive system components.
