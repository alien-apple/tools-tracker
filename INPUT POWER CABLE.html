AERO COMPOSITE CLUSTER

INPUT POWER CABLE

  • PART MODEL – HCS9000-70-145.
  • FUNCTION – The cable delivers AC power to the hot bonder, enabling the operation of its internal control circuitry, heating devices, and other components necessary for composite repair processes.
  • HOW IT WORKS – Once connected, the input power cable allows the hot bonder to energize its heating blankets or other heating elements, which are essential for curing composite materials at controlled temperatures.
