  • HOW IT WORKS- A hot bonder unit works by precisely controlling heat and vacuum to cure adhesive in a localized area for composite and metal repairs. It uses a flexible heating blanket to apply heat to the damaged section, while a vacuum bag system applies pressure to ensure good thermal contact, compaction, and removal of air and gases. The unit monitors and adjusts temperature using thermocouples to follow a specific cure profile, ensuring the adhesive or prepreg material cures correctly and restores structural integrity.
