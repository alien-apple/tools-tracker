AERO COMPOSITE CLUSTER

OUTPUT POWER CABLE

  • PART MODEL – HCS9000-72-175.
  • MAX LOAD – 7200 WATTS.
  • FUNCTION – The output power cable in a composite repair hot bonder system is responsible for transmitting electrical power from the hot bonder unit to the heating devices—typically composite curing heating blankets or similar elements—that are used to apply controlled heat to the repair area.
  • HOW IT WORKS – The output power cable delivers the regulated AC power from the hot bonder’s output connector directly to the heating blankets, enabling them to generate the necessary heat for curing composite materials.
  • ADVANTAGE – Heavy-Duty Construction – Designed to handle high current loads during composite repair cycles, ensuring safe and reliable power transfer. .
