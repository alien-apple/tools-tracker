AERO COMPOSITE CLUSTER

VACUUM GAUGE

  • PART MODEL – HCS2018.
  • FUNCTION – The vacuum gauge in a composite repair hot bonder system is used to measure and display the level of vacuum (negative pressure) applied to the repair area during the curing process.
  • HOW IT WORKS – The gauge provides a real-time indication of the vacuum level inside the vacuum bag or repair zone, ensuring that the required pressure is maintained throughout the cure cycle.
